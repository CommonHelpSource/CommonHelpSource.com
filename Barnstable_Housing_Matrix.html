Barnstable County Housing Matrix

  • Low-Income Housing: Apply via CHAMP and contact Housing Assistance Corporation.
  • Market-Rate Rentals: Use Cape Cod Rentals, Craigslist, and social media groups.
  • Emergency Shelter: Call 2-1-1 or contact the Housing Assistance Corporation directly.
  • Transitional Housing: Inquire through Duffy Health Center or local shelters.
  • Senior Housing: Contact Councils on Aging and check housing authority waitlists.
  • First-Time Homebuyers: Use MassHousing and Barnstable County homebuyer classes.
  • Subsidized Vouchers: Apply through Barnstable Housing Authority and HAC.
  • Tenant Rights: Contact South Coastal Counties Legal Services for help.
