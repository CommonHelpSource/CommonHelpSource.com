Bristol County Housing Matrix

1. Low-Income Housing

  1. Complete the CHAMP application: mass.gov/apply-for-public-housing
  2. Create an email address if needed to apply and get updates
  3. Submit required documents like ID and income proof
  4. Call local Housing Authorities to confirm they received your CHAMP application
  5. Check your CHAMP waitlist status monthly using your email and login

2. Market-Rate Rentals

  1. Search listings on Zillow, Trulia, HotPads, or Craigslist
  2. Filter by monthly rent, location, and number of bedrooms
  3. Check if landlord accepts Section 8 or other subsidies
  4. Prepare a rental resume with references, ID, and income verification
  5. Tour properties in person or virtually before signing anything

3. Emergency Shelter

  1. Call 2-1-1 or contact DHCD
  2. Explain your current situation and ask about emergency shelter eligibility
  3. Provide documents if needed (ID, eviction notice, etc.)
  4. If eligible, get placed in shelter and meet with a caseworker
  5. Ask about steps for long-term housing
