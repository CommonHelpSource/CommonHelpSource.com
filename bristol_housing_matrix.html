Bristol County Housing Matrix

  • Low-Income Housing: Apply via CHAMP and contact Fall River or New Bedford Housing Authorities.
  • Market-Rate Rentals: Search New Bedford Guide, Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist.
  • Emergency Shelter: Connect with Catholic Social Services for placements.
  • Transitional Housing: Look into programs for families or reentry support in the area.
  • Senior Housing: Contact Greater Attleboro-Taunton Regional Transit Authority for referrals.
  • First-Time Homebuyers: Attend local workshops via Neighborhood Housing Services.
  • Subsidized Vouchers: Apply to Taunton and Fall River Housing Authorities.
  • Tenant Rights: Use Southeastern Mass Legal Aid services and RAFT if behind on rent.
