Massachusetts Housing Matrix

Apply for Low-Income Housing

Step 1: Determine Eligibility

Check if your household income falls within the area median income (AMI) limits for your county. Generally, you should earn less than 80% of AMI.

Step 2: Gather Documents

Collect required documents:

  • Photo ID for all adult household members
  • Social Security cards for all household members
  • Birth certificates for all household members
  • Income verification (pay stubs, tax returns)
  • Bank statements

Step 3: Apply

Submit applications through:

  • Local Housing Authority
  • CHAMP (Common Housing Application for Massachusetts Public Housing)
  • Section 8 Centralized Waiting List

Find Market-Rate Rentals

Step 1: Set Your Budget

Calculate your maximum monthly rent (typically 30% of gross monthly income) and factor in utilities and other expenses.

Step 2: Search Listings

Use reliable sources:

  • Local real estate websites
  • Property management companies
  • Housing search websites
  • Local newspapers

Step 3: Prepare Application Materials

Have ready:

  • Credit report
  • Employment verification
  • References
  • First/last month's rent and security deposit

Explore Senior Housing Options

Step 1: Assess Needs

Consider:

  • Level of independence
  • Medical needs
  • Desired amenities
  • Location preferences

Step 2: Explore Options

Research available programs:

  • Senior public housing
  • Section 202 Supportive Housing
  • Assisted living facilities
  • Continuing care retirement communities

Step 3: Contact Resources

Reach out to:

  • Local Council on Aging
  • Elder services organizations
  • Senior housing complexes

First-Time Homebuyer Programs

Step 1: Financial Preparation

Take these initial steps:

  • Check your credit score
  • Save for down payment
  • Calculate affordable price range
  • Get pre-approved for a mortgage

Step 2: Education

Complete requirements:

  • First-time homebuyer education course
  • Financial literacy workshops
  • Housing counseling sessions

Step 3: Apply for Programs

Consider these options:

  • MassHousing loans
  • ONE Mortgage Program
  • FHA loans
  • Down payment assistance programs
