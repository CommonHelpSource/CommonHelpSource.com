Dukes County Housing Matrix

1. Affordable Housing

  1. Apply through Island Housing Trust
  2. Review eligibility guidelines
  3. Submit application and supporting documents
  4. Wait for lottery or direct assignment
  5. Ask about emergency grants or RAFT

2. Homeownership Assistance

  1. Check with local nonprofits for down payment help
  2. Take homebuyer education course
  3. Apply for ONE Mortgage if eligible
  4. Get pre-approved through lenders familiar with island market
  5. Attend open houses with a local agent
