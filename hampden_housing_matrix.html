Hampden County Housing Matrix

1. Low-Income Housing

  1. Complete the CHAMP application: mass.gov/apply-for-public-housing
  2. Create an email address if needed to apply and get updates
  3. Submit required documents like ID and income proof
  4. Call local Housing Authorities (Springfield, Holyoke, Chicopee) to confirm they received your CHAMP application
  5. Check your CHAMP waitlist status monthly using your email and login
