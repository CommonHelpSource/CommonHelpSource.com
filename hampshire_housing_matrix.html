Hampshire County Housing Matrix

1. Low-Income Housing

  1. Apply via CHAMP: mass.gov/apply-for-public-housing
  2. Reach out to Amherst, Northampton, or Belchertown Housing Authorities
  3. Ask local senior centers or churches for help completing forms
  4. Document your application submission and follow up after 2 weeks
  5. Keep login and tracking info in a safe place
