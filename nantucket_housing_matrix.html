Nantucket County Housing Matrix

1. Low-Income Housing

  1. Contact Nantucket Housing Office
  2. Complete CHAMP and local housing applications
  3. Submit income and residency documents
  4. Monitor waitlist communications
  5. Ask about short-term rental subsidies

2. Emergency Housing

  1. Call 211 or island-based social service orgs
  2. Request shelter or transitional housing referrals
  3. Explain urgent needs clearly
  4. Provide ID and eviction info
  5. Stay in touch with assigned caseworker
