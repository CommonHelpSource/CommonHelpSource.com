Suffolk County Housing Matrix

1. Low-Income Housing

  1. Apply via Boston Housing Authority or Metro Housing Boston
  2. Create an online CHAMP profile
  3. Upload verification documents
  4. Check your waitlist status regularly
  5. Contact local housing offices if needed

2. Market-Rate Rentals

  1. Use apps like Zillow, Trulia, and Rent.com
  2. Search by neighborhood preferences
  3. Ask about voucher acceptance
  4. Schedule property viewings
  5. Prepare documents before applying
