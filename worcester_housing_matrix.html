Worcester County Housing Matrix

First-Time Homebuyers

  1. Take a certified homebuyer class (WCHR, RCAP)
  2. Get pre-approval from a bank
  3. Explore ONE Mortgage or MassHousing
  4. Search within your pre-approved range
  5. Ask about down payment assistance

Tenant Rights / Eviction Help

  1. Contact Community Legal Aid: (855) 252-5342
  2. Visit https://www.masslegalhelp.org for legal info
  3. Apply for RAFT if behind on rent
  4. Respond quickly to eviction notices
  5. Work with a legal advocate if needed
